December Highlights

Tuesday, December 2, 2014

The big story this month is seeing AngularJS reach the top 5. To see it reach that in about 2 years is a stunning rise, even in in today's fast-moving tech industry.

In another interesting development, Passion, though not a tech term, is now ubiquitious, having reached the top 10. No passion? Don't bother applying with these copmanies I guess.

In other news, not surprisingly, JavaScript continued its streak of victories whith Python the runner up again.
