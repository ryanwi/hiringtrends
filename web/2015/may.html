May Highlights

Thursday, May 21, 2015

The most interesting trend from May is the continuing, quick rise of the React JavaScript library. While AngularJS was a pioneer of meteoric rises, React's may end up being a faster climb to the top of the JavaScript library war. It just zoomed past Backbone.js into 2nd place after just about a year since it was first mentiond in the hiring posts.
